Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Customer Support Agent ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you interact with our AI-powered messaging service on Facebook and Instagram.

1. Information We Collect

When you send a direct message to our Facebook Page or Instagram profile, we automatically collect the following information through the Meta Platforms API:

  • Your Message Content: The text and media you send to us.
  • Your User ID: A unique identifier provided by Meta, not your personal name.
  • Page/Profile ID: The identifier of our page you are contacting.
  • Conversation History: We store the history of our conversation to provide context-aware support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate our AI-powered customer service.
  • To understand and respond to your inquiries, requests, and comments.
  • To improve the accuracy and effectiveness of our AI agent's responses.

3. Data Processing and Third-Party Services

Our service utilizes several trusted third-party services to process your information for the purposes described above:

  • OpenAI: Your messages are processed by OpenAI's API (GPT models) to generate responses. OpenAI does not use data from our API to train its models. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • n8n.io: Our workflow automation platform that processes and routes messages. See n8n's Privacy Policy.
  • PostgreSQL Database: Conversation history is stored in a secure, encrypted database to maintain context.
  • Twilio: If a problem is detected (e.g., your message contains the word "problem"), an anonymous alert is sent to our internal team via WhatsApp for customer support purposes.

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your conversation history for a period of 90 days to provide continuous service. After this period, the data is automatically deleted from our systems.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at ai.agent.helpdesk@gmail.com.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ai.agent.helpdesk@gmail.com.
